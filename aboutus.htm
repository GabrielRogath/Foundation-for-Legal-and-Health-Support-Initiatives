About Us

Foundation for Legal and Health Support Initiatives was formed in 2021 by a small group of youth, aiming to enhance youth livelihood through health and legal to youth with drug addicts.

VISSION

To envisage a community where Youth potential are realized of utilized for National development.

MISSION

To enhance Youth well-being through legal aid and health initiatives for sustainable livelihood.

Objectives

  1. Promoting youth participation in the war against Poverty
  2. To enhance youth access to entrepreneurship skills
  3. To promote and raise awareness of human Rights in the society
  4. To promote access to health education to youth
  5. To sensitive the Community on existence transmission and protection of communicable diseases, Sexual Transmitted Diseases, Drugs, HIV/AIDS.

"We Founder Members have agreed to establish the Organization with the name Foundation for Legal and Health Support Initiatives to enhance youth livelihood through health and legal to youth with drug addicts."
